• BikeSharing360 is a smart bike sharing system with 10,000 bikes distributed in 650 stations located throughout New York City and Seattle.
How it Works

  • Book

    Find and book bikes and get personalized recommendations of events in your city. Get your bike, purchase tickets to events, and ride to them.

  • Discover

    Discover a fun and efficient new way to move around your city. Learn about new routes and get personalized recommendations on routes. Riding a bike and buyinh what you need on the road was never so easy and enjoyable.

  • Enjoy

    Leave your car in your garage and get fit while you enjoy and discover your city. Let BikeSharing360 suggest custom rides just for you and go where you need while you enjoy what you like.
